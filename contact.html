About BG1

Hi there! My name is Mark and I've helped make BG1 a better application. The app was originally written by Joel Bruick. It is excellent, but has some limitations that Joel deliberately left in place to mimic My Disney Experience.

For instance, a Genie+ request cannot be made prior to Disney's set time - even though it is possible to do. I removed those limitations.

If you like the updates I've made, you're welcome to support my work via either Venmo or PayPal.

I've left Joel's information below, as much of the work and effort is his.

Hi, I'm Joel! I've been a Disney theme park fan since my very first trip to Walt Disney World as a kid. When I'm not watching a WDW or Disneyland live stream, there's a good chance I'm listening to classic EPCOT background area music.

I created BG1 after reading endless complaints online from people who lost out on a chance to ride the groundbreaking new Rise of the Resistance attraction because of seemingly random technical issues with Disney's app. Many of the enhancements introduced by BG1 were later implemented in the official apps, creating a less frustrating experience for the millions of guests who visit Disney's parks every year.

Since then, I've updated BG1 to help guests get the most out of the Genie+ service, with a more user-friendly, streamlined interface for finding and booking the best Lightning Lanes.

If my work has helped make your Disney vacation extra magical, you're welcome to support my efforts using either Venmo or PayPal (no account required). Thanks in advance!
